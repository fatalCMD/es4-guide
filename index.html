Levels 1–5: Foundations

  • Complete tutorial, deliver Amulet of Kings to Jauffre (free Paint Horse & early fast travel).
  • Join the Arena. Win matches for fast gold (~500 per win) & Champion’s Raiment (solid starting armor).
  • Visit each Mages Guild for recommendations. Complete at least five to access Arcane University (unlocks enchanting, spell-making).
  • Commit murder to get Lucien Lachance’s invitation—join the Dark Brotherhood early for Shrouded Armor and stealth rewards.
  • Speak to the beggar at Imperial Waterfront (midnight): unlock Thieves Guild for fencing stolen loot.
  • Dawn/dusk near Cheydinhal, offer Glow Dust at Azura’s Shrine. Reward: Azura’s Star (reusable Grand Soul Gem).
  • Help defend Kvatch (~level 5–10) before random gates spawn for smoother scaling.

Levels 6–10: Early Questlines & Artifacts

  • Advance guild ranks via deliveries & rat hunts—train combat and reach halfway status by level 10.
  • Start Mages Guild Necromancer quest arc (recommended levels 8–10).
  • Retrieve the Eye of Nocturnal: get Skeleton Key (unbreakable lockpick, +40 Security).
  • With Personality ≤20, complete Namira’s quest: obtain Ring of Namira (major damage reflect).
  • Clear the Ruin for Meridia: reward is Ring of Khajiiti (chameleon & speed).
  • Begin Knights of the Nine pilgrimage for Boots of the Crusader (+calm creatures).

Levels 11–15: Mid-Game Climax

  • Finish lawful guild quests; embrace the Thieves/Dark Brotherhood side for endgame perks.
  • Fighters Guild: finish Blackwood plotline for Helm of Oreyn Bearclaw (+Agility/Endurance).
  • Mages Guild: defeat Mannimarco and claim Arch-Mage rank and spell perks.
  • Thieves Guild: pull off the Ultimate Heist (Gray Cowl = Detect Life, Feather bonus).
  • Dark Brotherhood: become Listener; earn Night Mother’s Blessings & Shadowmere.
  • Mephala: Win the Bleaker’s Way feud for Ebony Blade (Absorb Health sword).
  • Mehrunes Dagon: Plunder Varsa Baalim for Mehrunes’ Razor (insta-kill dagger).

Levels 16–20: Late-Game Preparation

  • Finish remaining Daedric quests (Boethiah, Clavicus, Molag Bal, Hircine); prep for 14 artifact collection.
  • Cross Niben for Shivering Isles but delay finale till level 25+ for best rewards.
  • Close the Bruma Great Gate (level 20 keeps allies competitive); retrieve the Sigil Stone.
  • Complete KotN storyline to resync relics after level-ups.

Levels 21–30+: Endgame & Isles

  • Reforge gear to max stats—legendary Daedric powers unlocked.
  • Finish Shivering Isles endgame for Madgod title.
  • Hermaeus Mora: after all Prince quests/artifact sacrifices, obtain +10 skill tome.
  • Complete side quests, customize estates, and bask in your many heroic titles: Hero, Arch-Mage, Listener, Gray Fox, Divine Crusader, Madgod.
